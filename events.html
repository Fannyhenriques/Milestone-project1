Events and Catering!

Whether you’re a larger party celebrating or a company planning an event, you’re more than welcome at Hospitable. We also offer catering if you’d like to host an event at home. We’re excited to announce that live music will be available every Thursday starting January 22nd. Book your table now, or contact us if you have any questions!

Large parties

For groups of 7–20, we offer long-table reservations in our dining room, perfect for celebrating together.You and your guests will enjoy a four-course, family-style set menu featuring some of our signature dishes and seasonal favorites. It’s a mix of small plates, sharing dishes, and heartier courses — with a cost of €65 per person.

Beverages are served from our a la carte evening menu and are charged separately, based on what you enjoy. We offer two reservation slots for larger parties: 6:00–8:30 PM and 9:00–11:30 PM. Let us take care of the food while you focus on the celebration! Please contact us for more information.

Offsite event catering

We also take Hospitable on the road! We’ll come to you and can cater events of all sizes and occasions. We create delicious, custom menus for weddings, private dinners, corporate events, and cocktail parties — bringing the best of Hospitable straight to your table.

Choose the style of event you want, and we’ll make it fun. We tailor our menus to your event and are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice. Thank you for considering Hospitable for your event. Reach out to us for booking and if you want to know more.
